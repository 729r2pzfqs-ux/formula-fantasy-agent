FAQ

Q: Is this an official F1 Fantasy app?
A: No, Formula Fantasy Agent is an unofficial companion app. It is not affiliated with Formula 1 or F1 Fantasy.

Q: How do I restore my Pro Pass subscription?
A: Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Your subscription is linked to your Apple ID.

Q: Where does the data come from?
A: We use publicly available F1 statistics and timing data to provide insights and recommendations.
