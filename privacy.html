Privacy Policy for Formula Fantasy Agent

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Formula Fantasy Agent is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or process personally identifiable information.

Data Collection

Formula Fantasy Agent does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Diagnostic or crash data
  • Location data
  • Tracking data

The app does not use cookies, tracking technologies, or third-party analytics services.

Use of Data

Formula Fantasy Agent displays Formula 1 statistics and fantasy racing insights based on publicly available F1 data. All data is presented locally in the app and is not linked to any individual user.

Third-Party Services

Formula Fantasy Agent does not share user data with third parties and does not include advertising, tracking SDKs, or external analytics tools.

In-App Purchases

Formula Fantasy Agent offers optional in-app purchases in the form of an auto-renewable subscription called Pro Pass.

All payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. Formula Fantasy Agent does not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. Subscription status and billing are managed entirely by Apple and linked to your Apple ID.

Children's Privacy

Formula Fantasy Agent does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Future Changes

If new features are introduced that affect user privacy (such as analytics, accounts, or advertising), this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features are enabled.
